# Derelict

Co-op browser survival horror. 

## Inspirations
- FTL
- Alien Isolation
- Mothership

## Build out
- attaches to a discord channel, posts little moves
- has a web ui where you see the map, can make moves
- something like turns are handed out on a cadence
- users can coordinate, but on a turn they can move and take an action
- Panic (mothership) engine stats
- scenario builder on the web ui

## Core Loop
- Actions handed out
- players determine their movement/action
- they navigate a ship/dungeon/etc fighting monsters, rolling dice against challenges
- their health and other stats go up and down

## Win/Loss Conditions
- They Survive, Solve or Save

## Player Goals
- Escape the horrors
- Save the innocents

- The map is a grid system with walls, doors, entities (obstacles, items, npcs, monsters)
